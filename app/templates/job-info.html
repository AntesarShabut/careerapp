Job description

Job Type: Full-Time

Department : User Experience (UX)

Reports to : Senior UX Designer / Product Manager

 

Job Overview:

 

We are seeking a passionate and detail-oriented **Entry Level UX Analyst** to join our growing UX team. In this role, you will work closely with senior designers, product managers, and developers to analyze and improve the user experience of our digital products. This position is ideal for recent graduates or individuals with a keen interest in user research, data analysis, and UX design principles. You will assist in conducting user research, interpreting data, and delivering actionable insights to enhance user interactions with our products.

 

Key Responsibilities :

 

- Assist in conducting user research through interviews, surveys, usability tests, and other methods to gather insights about user needs and behaviors.

- Analyze qualitative and quantitative data from user research to identify pain points and opportunities for improving the user experience.

- Create user journey maps, personas, and wireframes to help visualize user flows and product enhancements.

- Collaborate with UX designers and developers to ensure the implementation of user-centered design principles in the development of new features.

- Assist in the preparation and presentation of research findings to stakeholders and team members.

- Participate in competitive analysis to stay informed of industry trends and best practices.

- Help monitor product usability metrics and provide recommendations for continuous improvements.

- Support in maintaining and organizing research databases, design libraries, and documentation.

 

Required Skills & Qualifications:

 

- Bachelor’s degree in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Psychology, Design, or a related field.

- Basic knowledge of UX design principles and best practices.

- Familiarity with UX research methods, including usability testing, surveys, and interviews.

- Experience with design tools (e.g., Figma, Sketch, Adobe XD) and prototyping tools is a plus.

- Strong analytical skills and attention to detail.

- Ability to translate research findings into actionable design recommendations.

- Excellent communication skills, both written and verbal.

- Team player with a willingness to learn and grow in a fast-paced environment.

- Basic understanding of HTML, CSS, or other web technologies is a plus but not required.

 

Preferred Qualifications:

 

- Experience working on projects or internships related to UX design, user research, or data analysis.

- Familiarity with tools like Google Analytics, Hotjar, or other UX research tools is a plus.

- Passionate about solving user problems and improving product usability.

 

Benefits:

 

- Competitive salary and benefits package

- Mentorship and training opportunities for career growth

- Collaborative work environment with cross-functional teams

- Access to cutting-edge tools and resources

- Flexible working hours and remote work options
